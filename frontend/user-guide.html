Quick Start Checklist

1. Sign in and review dashboard health

After login, use Dashboard to confirm incident totals, risk distribution, and any assets with critical exposure.

2. Report and analyze security incidents

Open Report Issue, select an asset, and submit a detailed description. The workflow enriches data using scan context and CVE intelligence.

3. Track remediation in Incident Logs

Use filters by status and risk level, add progress notes, and keep ownership clear for each mitigation task.

4. Validate decisions in Risk Analysis

Open Risk Analysis to understand why a risk score is high and what controls to prioritize first.

5. Export reports for leadership and audit

Export incident and risk reports as CSV for leadership updates, audit evidence, and trend reviews.
