Settings

1. Sign In And Overview

Log in with your assigned account. Start from Dashboard to review total assets, active incidents, and risk highlights.

2. Report A Security Issue

Go to Report Issue, choose affected asset, provide description, and submit. The AI analysis and risk score are generated automatically.

3. Track Incident Progress

Use Incidents to review status updates, notes, and recommendations. Filter by risk level to prioritize your work quickly.

4. Review Risk Analysis

Open Risk Analysis for matrix, trends, and asset-level risk summaries. Export a report when preparing daily or weekly briefings.
