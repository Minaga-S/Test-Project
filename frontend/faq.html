FAQ

Common questions and quick answers

How do I report an incident?

Go to Report Issue, fill in the form, and submit. Minimum description length is required for analysis.

Why is my risk level high?

Risk is calculated from likelihood and impact. High-impact assets and severe threat types increase the score.

Can I export data?

Yes. Use export buttons in Incident Logs and Risk Analysis pages for CSV reports.

How do I reset my password?

Open Settings, then Password tab, enter current password and your new password.
