How do I report an incident?

Open Report Issue, select the affected asset, add a clear description, and submit. Include what happened, when it happened, and user impact so analysis quality improves.

Why did the scan step get skipped?

Scan is skipped when live scan is disabled for the asset or when the scan target is not configured. In that case, analysis continues using cached security context.

Why is my risk level high?

Risk is based on likelihood and impact. Exposed ports, vulnerable services, severe threats, and high-value assets increase final risk score.

Can I export incident or risk reports?

Yes. Use export actions on Incident Logs and Risk Analysis pages to download CSV reports for briefings and audits.

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings, open the password section, then enter your current password and new password. Use a strong unique password for security operations accounts.

What happens if AI analysis fails?

The platform can still use scan findings and NIST CVE enrichment. AI failure should not remove core scan data like ports, services, OS, and CPE context.
